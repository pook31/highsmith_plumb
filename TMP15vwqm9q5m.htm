The Plumber You've Been Looking For
When I first started my career I knew what I wanted to do. Run a small plumbing business with my family.

I was lucky and had a chance to work for some great plumbers. Some taught me how to be honest, hard working and always put the customer’s needs first. Some showed me how not to treat a customer. It gave me the opportunity to keep the good habits and avoid the bad business techniques that so often ruin customer relationships. I wanted to become the kind of plumber that helps my customer with their plumbing problems and keeps them as a customer.

Early on I noticed that most mom and pop (small family) plumbers that I worked for were named “Smith and Sons” or “Jones and Sons”. So I always figured that when the time came to start my own plumbing company I would name it “Highsmith and Sons”. As my family grew it seemed there was a small issue. Our family was blessed with two beautiful daughters. They are still young and I wanted them to know that I would be very proud to have them follow in my footsteps. So “Highsmith and Daughters Plumbing” was born.

My Goal
It is simple. I want to be your family’s plumber. I want your children and your grandchildren to be our customers. One day they will tell my daughters that we have been your family's plumbers for as long as they can remember.
